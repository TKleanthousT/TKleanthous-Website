Process Notes

A quick look at how I build a piece.

Starts & Studies

Thumbnails for composition, then value studies (graphite or digital). I keep early passes loose to plan edges and focal points.

Color Decisions

Limited palettes first; hue variation comes later. Acrylic layers move fast—gouache will help me refine soft transitions.

Finish & Export

For digital, I export PNG/TIFF for print. For traditional, I photograph.
